TALE OF THE NEXUS

In the dawn of Rialo, builders gathered at the edge of a new chain.

They came searching for a place where signal outweighs noise.

From their hands rose the Nexus. One dashboard, seven halls.

In the first hall, daily check ins guard the streak. Each return is remembered.

In the second, missions wait. Likes, retweets, follows, chats. Every action turns into NEXUS.

A third hall holds the badges. Bronze for the early, silver for the steady, gold for the relentless.

The leaderboard names the loudest builders. The chatroom keeps them close.

The X tracker watches every post that mentions RialoHQ, so no signal is lost.

The last hall belongs to you alone. Your profile. Your record. Your name carried into the Rialo era.

The road is open.

Earn your place. The Nexus remembers.
